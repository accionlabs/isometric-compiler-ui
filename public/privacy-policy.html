Privacy Policy for SVG Isometric Shapes Compiler

Last updated: October 25, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SVG Isometric Shapes Compiler ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our web application and services.

Information We Collect

Google Drive Integration Data

  • Access to specific Google Drive folders and files that you explicitly authorize
  • Spreadsheet data containing shape library information
  • SVG files stored in your designated Google Drive folders
  • File metadata (names, IDs, and locations)

Application Data

  • Diagram compositions and layouts created within the application
  • Canvas settings and preferences
  • File names and folder paths for saved diagrams
  • Application settings and configurations

Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system information
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage data and interaction patterns

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  1. Provide and maintain the SVG Isometric Shapes Compiler service
  2. Save and load your diagram compositions
  3. Manage your shape libraries
  4. Improve and optimize the application's performance
  5. Respond to technical support requests
  6. Maintain application security

Data Storage and Security

Google Drive Data

  • We do not store your Google Drive files on our servers
  • All Google Drive interactions are performed through secure OAuth 2.0 authentication
  • File access is limited to the specific folders and files you authorize

Local Storage

  • Application preferences and settings are stored in your browser's local storage
  • No personal data is stored on our servers

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or property
  • To prevent fraud or abuse
  • With your explicit consent

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  1. Access your data
  2. Modify your application settings
  3. Revoke Google Drive access permissions
  4. Delete your local application data
  5. Opt-out of non-essential data collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Providing in-app notifications of significant changes
